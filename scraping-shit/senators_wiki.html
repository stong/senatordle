From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This article is about senators serving in the 119th United States Congress. For a list of senators in U.S. state legislatures, see List of U.S. state senators.
"List of United States senators" redirects here. For previous senators, see List of former United States senators.

The United States Senate consists of 100 members, two from each of the 50 states. This list includes all senators serving in the 119th United States Congress.
